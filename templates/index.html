Calculation Rules

  • Daily Salary

    Basic salary divided by number of days in selected month

  • Absent Days

    Full day salary deducted for each absent day

  • Late Entry

    First 3 days free, then half day deduction for each additional day

  • Overtime

    Half day salary added for each overtime day

  • Provident Fund

    12% of basic salary (maximum on ₹15,000)

  • ESI

    0.75% of basic salary (only if basic ≤ ₹21,000)

  • Professional Tax

    Based on salary slabs (₹0 to ₹200)
